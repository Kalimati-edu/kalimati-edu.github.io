Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 6, 2025

Kalimati ("the App") helps you learn German vocabulary. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Process

  • Device and usage data (e.g., device identifiers, app version, ad-related identifiers) used for app functionality, analytics, and advertising.
  • Advertising: We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Depending on your region and consent, ads may be personalized or non‑personalized.
  • Purchases: We use RevenueCat to manage in‑app purchases and entitlements (e.g., removing ads). RevenueCat may process purchase receipts and identifiers to deliver your subscription/entitlement.
  • Notifications: If you enable reminders, we store a simple preference to send local notifications.

Consent in EEA/UK/CH

In the European Economic Area, the UK, and Switzerland, we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ask for consent. You can change your choices at any time from the app’s privacy settings (where available).

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the App’s features (lessons, quizzes, progress).
  • Show ads and measure ad performance (via Google Mobile Ads).
  • Process purchases and manage entitlements (via RevenueCat).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We share data with service providers only to operate the App, for example:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) – advertising and related measurement.
  • RevenueCat – subscriptions and purchase validation.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, and as required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes
